Objective
---------

The objective of **FORTUNA** is to establish a building inventory for large-scale hazard assessment and simulation studies.

- The FORTUNA framework is structured as illustrated in the following image.

.. image:: fram.png
   :alt: FORTUNA framework
   :align: center
   :width: 400px

Accordingly, **FORTUNA** is built upon three main components: **data fusion**, **feature enhancement**, and **data imputation**, with the overarching goal of ensuring both **comprehensive feature representation** and **complete entity coverage**.
